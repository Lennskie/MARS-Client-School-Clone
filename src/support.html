Frequently Asked Questions

Who comes to my rescue?

A team composed of an emergency pilot, a senior medic, a sanitary assistant and a security operator. Our aerial vehicles come equipment with a state of the art cryomedic tank to stabilize your body until it arrives to the proper medical facilities.

What health vitals are being monitored?

There are four primary vital signs: body temperature, blood pressure, pulse (heart rate), and breathing rate (respiratory rate).
